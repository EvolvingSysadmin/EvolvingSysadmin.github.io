Background

I'm a solo IT and security practitioner who has spent 10+ years running the full stack — network infrastructure, identity management, endpoint security, incident response, and compliance — for a multi-site industrial manufacturer operating under a government subcontract. I hold a CISSP and completed an M.S. in Cybersecurity at Georgia Tech in May 2025, with a focus on policy.

My graduate practicum produced a set of 36 open-source cybersecurity policy templates aligned to NIST CSF 2.0, freely available for SMBs. Outside of work I'm based in the Eastern Sierra — trail running, alpine climbing, and fly fishing when I'm not in front of a terminal.
